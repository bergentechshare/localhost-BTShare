What is BT Share?

An opprotunity for students to further share their work

At Bergen Tech, students can explore a wide range of subjects through research and projects. Despite the time and effort put into these projects, often many months, many projects aren't publicly accessible due to limited publishing options. BT Share aims to address this by providing an online platform for students to share their work.
(This is NOT an offical publishing platform)
